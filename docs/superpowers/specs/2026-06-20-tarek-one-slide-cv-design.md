# Tarek Atwan One-Slide CV Design

## Objective

Create a polished, editable 16:9 portfolio slide that summarizes who Tarek Atwan is, the scale and range of his experience, major work, and representative clients. It should carry more information than the existing intro slide while remaining readable during a presentation.

## Audience

The slide must work equally well for executive consulting buyers, training and speaking buyers, universities, conferences, and enterprise audiences.

## Approved Direction

Use the approved Four-Pillar Executive Portfolio layout:

- A deep-navy identity rail on the left.
- A warm-ivory information field on the right.
- Muted gold accents and dividers.
- The existing circular portrait treatment.
- Four portfolio pillars: Advise, Build, Teach, and Publish.
- Build is the featured full-width row, split between consulting delivery and founder-builder proof.
- Advise, Teach, and Publish form a compact three-column row beneath Build.
- A compact client strip at the bottom.

The visual system must match the previously approved dark and light profile slides.

Place Advise, Teach, and Publish in a shorter three-column row below. This gives delivery and product execution more space without abandoning the four-part portfolio story.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The slide communicates identity, credibility, and range within ten seconds.
- The four pillars remain distinct and readable at presentation scale.
- The title and metric labels do not wrap unexpectedly.
- No content overlaps, clips, or extends beyond the slide bounds.
- The portrait and palette visibly belong to the existing personal brand system.
- The final PowerPoint opens as a valid one-slide presentation.
