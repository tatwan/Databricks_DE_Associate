# Tarek Atwan Profile Slide: Light Companion

## Objective

Create a light-background companion to the approved dark profile intro slide. The two versions must function as one personal brand system and be interchangeable across presentations.

## Design Direction

- Preserve the dark slide's 16:9 geometry, portrait placement, content hierarchy, metrics, expertise line, and footer.
- Use a warm-white canvas rather than stark white.
- Use deep navy for primary typography and structural lines.
- Retain muted gold for the identity kicker, metric values, portrait ring, and small accents.
- Use cool blue-gray for supporting copy and separators.
- Keep the presentation editorial, premium, serious, and technology-literate.

## Content

No wording or metric changes from the approved dark slide:

- Tarek Atwan
- AI & Data Advisor · Educator · Author
- Consultant / Educator / Author
- Positioning statement about turning AI ambition into production capability and building professional judgment
- Generative AI, Machine Learning, Data Engineering, Cloud, and Cybersecurity
- 20+ years, 85+ courses, 47+ enterprise clients, and 4 books
- tarekatwan.com and geographic coverage

## Deliverables

- One editable light-version PowerPoint slide.
- One combined editable presentation containing the dark and light variants.
- Rendered PNG previews for visual verification.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The light version visibly belongs to the same system as the dark version.
- All text remains readable at presentation distance.
- No unintended overlaps, clipping, or wrapping.
- The portrait remains prominent and professionally framed.
- Both slides are editable and export successfully.
